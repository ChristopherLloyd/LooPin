Other interesting classes of multiloops

  • The main page contains all multiloops with 12 or fewer regions for which the minimal region-degree is strictly greater than 2 (necessarily 3). There are only 6 of them.
  • The class of multisimple loops is also interesting. There are 54 of them with 12 or fewer regions.
